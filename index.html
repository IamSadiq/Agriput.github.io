Who we are, and what we're about!

AGRIPUT is an agro-input solutions company, we are all about our farmers and input providers, and we exist soley to serve them. We believe access to valid inputs is key to achieving better yields, and as such we are providing such services to help farmers' improve their farming and in the long-run have the revenues they deserve.

We provide verification services for farners' agricultural inputs, from agro-chemicals to seeds etc. to ensure that they have got and are using only authentic inputs on the farms, because only then will they be able to have better yields and hence increase their revenues.

We have partered with various agricultural input providers, standard boards and other Telecommunication access providers.
